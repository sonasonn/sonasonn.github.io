CANADA
The Maritimes, St. Lawrence River and Seaway, British Colombia
We can provide a full range of fuel oils, marine diesel and gas oil at Canadian bunker ports through our long association with local refiners and bunker suppliers. Supplies can be arranged by Bominflot in Newfoundland, Nova Scotia, New Brunswick, Quebec, the St. Lawrence Seaway and British Colombia. While barge delivery is often available, supply of all grades by truck is common and efficient in many ports in Eastern Canada and some locations offer ex-wharf supply. Supplies can also be arranged in the St. Lawrence Seaway to vessels arriving at or leaving the Great Lakes.
 
UNITED STATES
Houston (TX) and surrounding area
We supply a full range of fuel oils and distillates ex-pipeline to vessels working cargo at our dedicated storage facility at Magellan Midstream Partners Terminal (formerly Williams Energy Services), Galena Park (TX). Barge deliveries are made to vessels working cargo in the Houston Ship Channel and other ports, including Galveston (TX), Texas City (TX), Freeport (TX), Port Arthur (TX), Beaumont (TX), Lake Charles (LA) and, weather conditions permitting, at Bolivar Roads anchorage. Gas oil can also be delivered by truck subject to access. We are also able to arrange supply of a full range of bunker fuels in Corpus Christi (TX). Delivery is ex-wharf at oil terminal docks with appropriate facilities or by barge. Gas oil can also be supplied by truck (subject to access).
New Orleans (LA) and the lower Mississippi River
We deliver a full range of fuel oils and distillates from our terminal at John W. Stone Oil Distributing Co., only 30 minutes transit time by barge from the 9-12 miles anchorages. We also service all other bunkering locations along the 373 km navigable length of the Mississippi River from Pilottown to Baton Rouge, including the Industrial Canal. Gas oil is supplied by barge or by truck (subject to access).
Philadelphia (PA) and surrounding area
Our storage is located at Magellan Midstream Partners Terminal (formerly Delaware Terminal Co.) in Wilmington (DE). From there, we can deliver all grades of bunkers by barge or truck (subject to accessibility) to vessels working cargo in Philadelphia (PA), Paulsboro (NJ), Camden (NJ), Gloucester (NJ), Wilmington (DE), Delaware City (DE), and to anchorages at Marcus Hook, Mantua Creek, and Kaign’s Point.
Norfolk (VA) and surrounding area
Working from storage at IMTT Terminal in Chesapeake (VA), we can deliver all grades of bunkers by barge or truck to vessels within the greater Chesapeake Bay, including Norfolk, Portsmouth (VA), Newport News, Hampton Roads, Sewells Point and Yorktown. Ex-pipe deliveries are also possible at IMTT Chesapeake.
Baltimore (MD)
The port is accessible by barge either from the Delaware River (via the Chesapeake and Delaware Canal), or from Norfolk (via Chesapeake Bay). With storage strategically located at both ends of the range, Bominflot is uniquely positioned to offer competitively-priced products in the ports of Baltimore, Sparrows Point, and Cove Point (MD).
Other ports
Our Group's long association with refiners and bunker suppliers in all other areas within the United States enables Bominflot to provide fuel in all other bunker ports in New England, New York, New Jersey, Maryland, Georgia, Florida, Alabama, California, Oregon and Washington. Supplies are generally made by barge although gas oil is available by truck (subject to access).
 
CENTRAL AMERICA / CARIBBEAN
Caribbean Islands
Our long association with the Caribbean Island refiners and bunker suppliers allows us to provide bunkers throughout the region wherever they are available. Gas oil is available in most locations while a full range of bunker qualities can be supplied in the larger islands, particularly where oil is refined and/or stored.
Mexico
Bominflot enjoys a close association with the Mexican state oil company. As a result, we are able to offer competitively-priced bunkers at all bunker locations on the Caribbean and Pacific coasts of Mexico, offering a viable alternative to Panama, California and some US Gulf Coast locations. Deliveries are available ex-pipe at oil installations and, in several ports, by barge. Gas oil can be supplied by truck subject to accessibility.
Panama
Bominflot provides bunkers at either end of the Panama Canal in Balboa or Cristobal. A full range of fuel oils together with gas oil is available. Marine diesel is available from time to time. Deliveries can be made ex-pipe and by barge in both ports.

IFO 380 cSt, IFO 180 cSt, IFO 120 cSt, distillate MDO and gas oil can be supplied at Buenos Aires, Buenos Aires Roads (Zona Com?n), La Plata, Campana and Zarate.

All grades of bunker fuels(from 30 cSt up to 380 cSt) and gas oil are available from the sole refinery at Bahia Blanca, EG3 S.A. Through Deltamar S.A., who owns and operates a tanker in that area, we can arrange bunker deliveries in port (Ingeniero White/Puerto Galv·n/Puerto Belgrano) and at roads (Buoy 17).
Other ports
We can also arrange bunker deliveries in other Argentine ports: San Lorenzo, Rosario, San Nicol·s, Mar del Plata, Puerto Madryn, Puerto Deseado and Ushuaia.
 
BRASIL
Rio de Janeiro, Santos and other ports
Our Rio de Janeiro office has a long-standing contract with Petrobras. Thanks to this, competitive quotations are available upon request for a full range of fuel oils and marine gas oil for most ports.
 
OTHER SOUTH AMERICAN COUNTRIES
Uruguay, Chile, Peru, Ecuador, Colombia
A range of fuel oils and distillates can be provided at all ports from which bunkers are available at competitive prices due to Bominflot Buenos Aires' well-established connections with local bunker companies.
Venezuela, Curacao
Bominflot Rio de Janeiro's long-standing relationship with refiners and suppliers in Venezuela and Curacao means that we continue to be able to provide fuel oils and distillates in all bunker ports and at various locations offshore along the Venezuelan coast.
