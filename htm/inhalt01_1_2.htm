ESTONIA / BALTIC PORTS
Tallinn, Muuga, all other Estonian ports
Bominflot has its own products stored at a tank terminal with blending equipment. Thanks to its dedicated barge the 'Keila', Bominflot Estonia can supply all grades of high-quality marine fuel oil, diesel oil (DMB/DMA), gas oil 0.2 and lubricants.
Riga, Ventspils, Liepaja, Latvia, Klaipeda, Lithuania
Bominflot has long-standing relationships with the local refiners and suppliers in these ports and can offer competitive bunker prices. Most grades of fuel oil, marine diesel oil and gas oil are available meeting current ISO specifications.
 
FRANCE
Fos s. Mer, Lavera, Port St. Louis, Port de Bouc, Marseilles, Toulon, Nice, Monaco
Bominflot is able to supply a range of fuel oils and gas oil at each of these locations on the French Mediterranean coast. Deliveries are by barge, truck and in some locations by pipeline. Gas oil only can be supplied at Sete, La Nouvelle, Port Vendres and along the River Rhone as far inland as Lyon.
 
GERMANY
Hamburg, Elbehafen, Stade, Cuxhaven
Bominflot owns and operates a 250,000 cbm tank farm with a total of 75 tanks handling storage. Supply can be arranged using a fleet of chartered barges in the port of Hamburg, incl. Stade and up the river Elbe at Elbehafen and Cuxhaven. Tank farm facilities include five jetties for seagoing vessels with a max 80,000 DWT, discharge of railcars, truck racks, slops disposal and fresh water supply.
Kiel Canal
Bominflot Holtenau terminal has a total storage capacity of approx. 24,000 cbm. The 475 m berth has five bunker stations, two are equipped with blending equipment working 24 hours a day, 7 days a week. Deliveries are made ex-pipeline only.
Bremerhaven, Bremen, Nordenham, Brake, Wilhelmshaven and Elsfleth
The Bominflot tank farm is situated in Bremerhaven and is comprised of 13 tanks ranging from 500 cbm up to 18,000 cbm. One own barge and a fleet of private bunker barges are used to cover all deliveries ex Bremerhaven terminal to the above ports. All grades of fuel oil and distillates are available.
Rostock
All grades of fuel oil are available from physical stocks via the Grosstanklager Ölhafen Rostock (GÖR) tank storage terminal (capacity of 25,000 cbm). We have three bunker barges: two with a capacity of 1,000 Mts. and one with 600 Mts. Supplies can also be arranged in all German Baltic ports. Deliveries can also be made at Rostock roads or further offshore with "no agents or calling costs incurred".
 
GREECE
Piraeus
All grades of fuel oil are available at the port of Piraeus delivered by barge. From distillate products only gas oil is available and it is dyed black following customs regulations. Gas oil can be delivered by truck in specific docks. Bominflot Hellas can provide round the clock service at very competitive prices through established contacts with the local refineries and suppliers as well as competitive quotations for all major lubricant brands on a spot or contract basis for Greek ports and worldwide.
Thessaloniki
In the port of Thessaloniki only gas oil is available and can only be delivered by truck.
 
HOLLAND, BELGIUM
Dutch and Belgian ports
All grades of fuel oil and distillates are available from supply contracts from refineries and private storage in Rotterdam/Europoort, delivered by barges which are vetted and aproved by Bominflot BV Rotterdam.
 
RUSSIA
All ports
Bominflot has long-standing relationships with the local refiners and suppliers in these ports and can offer competitive bunker prices. Most grades of fuel oil, marine diesel oil and gas oil are available meeting current ISO specifications.
St. Petersburg
In 2003 Bominflot and Nevsky Mazut have established a cooperation to supply all bunker grades in St. Petersburg. All marketing and sales via Bominflot Hamburg.
Novorossiysk
Bominflot has opened its own office providing its own fuel oil of 380cst, 180 cSt and gas oil DMA. The company owns one dedicated barge for delivery and can supply ex-pipe tankers working cargo at the oil terminal.
Russian Black Sea, Ukraine
Fuel oil 380 and 180 cSt and gas oil are generally available and can be provided by Bominflot in Tuapse, Odessa, Ilichevsk and Yuzhyy.
 
SCANDINAVIA
Finland, Sweden, Norway, Denmark, Great Belt
Long-established relationships with local suppliers and refiners enable Bominflot to provide a range of fuel oils and distillates as locally available throughout this region.
 
SPAIN
Mainland Spain
Bominflot can provide bunker services through contracts with local refiners and bunker suppliers. In addition, Bominflot can offer other related services such as marine lubricants, ship chandlery, marine paints, chemicals and agency at competitive prices.
Ceuta
Bominflot stores its own products at the local installation. Ex-wharf supplies are possible to ships up to 220 m overall length and 33 ft maximum draft. Ships of minimum overall length 125 m can be supplied by barge in the port. In addition, Bominflot has its own agency division servicing vessels working cargo and vessels calling for bunkers only. Pricing is based upon vessel's gross registered tonnage.
Gibraltar
Bominflot set up an office and bunker supply operation in Gibraltar in January 2001. Supplies are conducted through fixed supply agreements with major oil companies. Together with the Spanish agency and warehousing group Maritima Del Estrecho, Bominflot jointly operates MT "Teisaro" for gas oil deliveries. Full agency services are also offered.
Las Palmas
Bominflot stores its own products at the local installation and can supply all grades IFO, MDO and gas oil ex-wharf or by barge. Bominflot is the official agent for Esso Lubricants. Competitive quotations for ExxonMobil and other luboil brands as well as full agency services can also be offered.
 
TURKEY
Istanbul and Sea of Marmara Ports
Long-established relationships with local suppliers and refiners enable Bominflot to provide a range of fuel oils and distillates as locally available throughout this region.
Turkish Black Sea, Georgia
Gas oil is available and can be provided by Bominflot.
 
EGYPT
All ports
Bominflot has long-standing contractual arrangements with MISR Petroleum and Copetrol and Caltex Egypt. Competitive quotations are available upon request. Bominflot can also offer canal transit and agency via Dominion Shipping Agencies. All grades of fuel oil are available at Suez and Port Said; in Alexandria only 180 cSt and lower viscosities.
 
KENYA
Mombasa
Bominflot is able to supply all grades of IFO and MGO in Mombassa port by barge or ex-pipe. Supplies can also be arranged off port limits.
 
SOUTHERN AFRICA
All ports in South Africa, Namibia and Mozambique
Bominflot South Africa commenced activities on 3rd December 2001 and is able to arrange supply in all South African ports, Namibia and Mozambique. Stock grades of IFO in South Africa are 180 cSt fuel oil (RMF25), MDO (DMB) and MGO (DMA). Lighter grades of fuel oil are possible, but subject to enquiries.
