CHINA
All ports
Bominflot arranges bunker deliveries at all bunker ports in China through long association with local companies. Generally, fuel oil 380 cSt is available at Ningbo, fuel oil 180 cSt is available at all ports except Xiamen. Light diesel oil is available at all ports, with 120 cSt fuel oil only available at Xingang.
 
HONG KONG
Inner and outer harbour
Bunkers are supplied by our local subsidiary in Hong Kong at the inner or outer harbour. Fuel oil from 30 cSt up to 380 cSt, MDO and Gas Oil are available, with fresh water deliverable to vessels at the outer harbour calling for bunkers only.
 
INDIA
Main ports
Well situated in the city of Mumbai, Bominoil can endeavour to supply bunker fuels in the main ports of India, preferably with five days‘ notice.
 
JAPAN
Honshu, Kyushu, Shikoku, Hokkaido and other ports
Bominflot provides a full range of fuel oils, marine diesel and gas oil at most ports in Honshu, Kyushu, Shikoku and Hokkaido thanks to long-term contracts with the local Japanese refineries. Oil fence charges may apply at some private berths.
 
MALAYSIA, PHILIPPINES, THAILAND, VIETNAM
Various ports
Malaysia, Philippines, Thailand and Vietnam are also coordinated through Bomin Singapore, which can arrange bunker supply at various ports.
 
SAUDI ARABIA
Jeddah, Dammam, Ras Tanura
Bominflot has a supply and agency co-operation with one of the largest bunker and agency companies in these ports. All grades of IFO up to a max viscosity of 180 cSt are available.
 
SINGAPORE
In-port, out-of-port
Through a long-standing presence that spans for twenty years, Bomin Singapore has reached the position of one of the top ten physical suppliers in Singapore, supplying all grades of fuel and distillates through its own or chartered barges. Supply can be made at anchorage, in-port, or out-of-port limits, and other services - such as Agency - are equally available to our customers.
 
SOUTH KOREA
Main ports
A recent acquisition, Bomin Seoul has distinguished itself by its quality service, offering supplies of a full range of fuel and distillates in the main ports of Korea at competitive prices.
 
SRI LANKA
Colombo, Trincomalee
Bominflot can arrange a full range of bunker fuels in Colombo and the port of Trincomalee by barges and ex-pipeline.
 
TAIWAN
All main ports
Bominflot maintains a close relationship with local national refiners and bunker supplier. Bunkers can be provided at competitive prices at all bunker ports in Taiwan, i.e. Kaohsiung, Keelung, Taichung, Hualien and Suao.
 
UNITED ARAB EMIRATES
Fujairah and other ports
Having established a joint venture with the Government of Fujairah, Bominflot has commenced deliveries of a full range of fuels and distillates in port and at anchorage. Through our office in Fujairah we are also able to arrange supply in other ports of the U.A.E.
 
YEMEN REPUBLIC
Aden
We can supply 380 cSt, 180 cSt and gas oil ex-pipeline, either at the refinery or at the commercial berths. Gas oil can also be supplied by barge. We can also provide a full agency service for vessels either working cargo or calling for bunkers only.
